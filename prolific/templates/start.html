Welcome to an Ai2 Prolific Survey!

We are a team of researchers working on improving the usefuless of AI models on video inputs.

To this end, we are collecting short clips that contain skateboarding actions.

We have sourced videos from YouTube and used existing AI models to identify potential clips.

You will help us verify the trimming of these clips. In particular, you will help ensure that:

  • Each clip contains exactly one skateboarding action.
  • The start and end times of each clip do not cut off parts of the skateboarding action.
  • Each clip is not significantly longer than it needs to be.

Note that the trimmings do not need to be perfect — it's ok for a clip to be a second or two longer than it needs to be.
